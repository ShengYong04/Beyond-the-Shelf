About Us

Beyond the Shelf. Co is a business based upon the selling of various books at a low price through our website

Our team consists of over 30 talented individuals who work round the clock to make sure our services are always top of the line!

Each and every book we sell come from hand-picked selections by publishers all over the world that are enjoyed by like-minded bookworms such as you. The books are found in English, Chinese and Malay, and cover a myriad of topics.
Cook books? We got them. Novels? We got them. Comics? You best believe we have those too!

Our Vision to create a smarter world

   Beyond the Shelf has dedicated itself to the eradication of illiteracy in Malaysia. That is why our books are sold at a lower price than most other bookstores, allowing even the most unfortunate to have a chance at picking up new reading materials. We hope that the ease of obtaining books will foster a habit of reading within the people, creating a nation of knowledgeable, critical thinking intellectuals. Thus, transforming the country into one that is capable of catching up with the other developing countries, and perhaps one day rivaling the most developed nations.
